About Horse Haven

SILKENO Horse Haven is dedicated to finding loving homes for horses in need. We rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome horses of all breeds and ages, ensuring they find their perfect forever families.

Our mission is to provide a second chance for horses who have been abandoned, neglected, or surrendered, and to match them with caring adopters who can provide the love and care they deserve.
